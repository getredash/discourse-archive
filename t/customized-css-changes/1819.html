Hey John,

Yes, that’s totally doable on your self-hosted instance.

I’d recommend starting from v4.0 (your company might be running on previous versions) as the design has been massively improved on the recent version: https://blog.redash.io/improving-redash-user-experience-36e3ca47d1b3

Please check out https://redash.io/help/open-source/dev-guide/setup for further instructions to set up your dev env locally.
