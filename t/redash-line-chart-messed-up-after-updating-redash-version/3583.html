Hi Itay_a.

I had the same issue in my upgrade, from v 5.0.2 to 7.0.0.
I think the “Category” now is really string sort, while was different in the past, that’s why you have “1,10,11,2,20,…”.

Either you switch to Linear, or leave Category and sort the query, so you can configure the chart not to sort the data.
