I think you’ve hit this behavior, which happens if you’re grouping data that’s not complete.

Custom sorting is overriden when stacked bars don't contain the same categories Support
Makes sense! Plotly draws records in the order it encounters them in your data set. Initially the data gets split by your grouping field so your one table becomes two: Blue time mintime category value 20-Jan 31/01/2020 00:00 Blue - 20-Apr 17/04/2020 10:46 Blue 6.04 20-May 1/5/20 10:23 Blue 18.5 20-Jun 1/6/20 9:43 Blue 8.43 20-Jul 1/7/20 6:38 Blue 17.2 20-Aug 3/8/20 6:56 Blue 16 20-Sep 1/9/20 7:11 Blue 8.56 Red time mintime category value 20-Feb 14/02/2020 17:33 Re…

To solve it, make sure there is at least one record for every x-axis value and group. Or just sort your data chronologically in the underlying sheet.
