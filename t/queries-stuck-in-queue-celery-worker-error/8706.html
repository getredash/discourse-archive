jesse July 9, 2021, 12:35am 5

There should be a comma between scheduled_queries and schemas.

Like this:

 scheduled_worker:
    <<: *redash-service
    command: worker
    environment:
      <<: *redash-environment
      QUEUES: "scheduled_queries,schemas"
      WORKERS_COUNT: 1
