This is a Plotly bug and not something Redash controls. The solution to widen the view port works because Plotly re-renders on <div> resize.

andyc:

its feels like such a basic piece of functionality

Not really. Plotly has had this bug for years (see the dozens of legend-overlap issues on their Github repo). Many talented developers tried. It’s not easy to solve.

One improvement we could implement is to allow arbitrary legend positions in visualization settings. Right now it’s automatic. This often breaks responsive layout. But maybe that’s a decision we could leave to the user.
