Hi @shakv, please don’t tag users on the forum unless you’re already in a conversation with them We already monitor this forum each day. Tagging us just creates duplicate notifications for us to sort through.

Refreshes happen on the hour. Whether you set the refresh schedule at 22 minutes past or 42 minutes past, it will try to refresh around 00 minutes.
